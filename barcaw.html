The Legacy of FC Barcelona

Més que un club (More than a club)

Futbol Club Barcelona, founded in 1899, embodies Catalan pride and beautiful football. With 5 UEFA Champions League titles and 27 La Liga championships, Barça's blue-and-claret colors represent a unique football philosophy.

Camp Nou has witnessed football artistry from generations of legends who perfected the "tiki-taka" style. From Cruyff to Messi, Barcelona's jersey carries the weight of genius.
